Odin Quesadillas

Quesadillas

Description

Easy yet delicious meal to make in under 5 minutes! You can never go wrong with quesadillas. Try them with chicken, beef, veggies or any other ingredient of your choice.

Ingredients:

  • flour tortillas (or corn tortillas, if you want a more "mexican taste")
  • cheese of your choice (preferably the kind that melts)
  • extra ingredient of your choice (ham, beef, chicken, spinach, etc.)

Steps:

  1. Heat up a pan.
  2. When the pan is hot place the tortilla on it and wait for it to warm up.
  3. Once the tortilla is warm, put the desired amount of cheese on it and wait for it to start melting.
  4. At this stage, you can fold the tortilla in half. If you want to add extra ingredients do so before folding the tortilla.
  5. Repeat the process to make as many quesadillas as you want.
  6. Enjoy!
